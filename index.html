This is how to make a peppermint latte:

  • • Brew your espresso (double shot)
  • • Steam up & froth milk
  • • Add 1/4 teaspoon of peppermint extract to brewed espresso
  • • Add half a teaspon of sugar to milk>
  • • Optional: Quarter teaspoon of vanilla extract
